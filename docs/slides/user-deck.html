Your Data, Your Rules

Offline-first.
No vendor lock-in.

Your notes are yours — stored locally, exportable anytime, readable forever as Markdown.

💾

localStorage persistence

Everything saved instantly to your browser's local storage. No network request. Ever.

📤

Export as Markdown

Download any page as a .md file. Open in any editor, migrate anywhere, anytime.

Works offline

No internet? No problem. Tracker is a local-first app — works on flights, in the woods, everywhere.
